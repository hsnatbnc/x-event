AI Product Showcase

X Event partnered with an AI technology company to design and develop a modern website for its intelligent product platform. The goal was to create a clean, future-facing experience that clearly presented the product's strengths and value.

Services: Concept development, web design, web development, 3D design, WebGL

Highlights: A sleek presentation approach with a strong focus on product storytelling and user engagement.

Luxury Motion Film

X Event was invited to produce a CG-driven short film for a premium automotive campaign focused on design heritage and visionary thinking.

The creative direction followed a four-stage visual journey, guiding viewers through the evolution of performance and design by using digital art, motion graphics, and cinematic storytelling. The final work was also designed to suit large-scale display environments and immersive presentation formats.

Services: Creative concept, 3D design, motion design, compositing

Highlights: Narrative-led visuals, premium motion language, and large-format presentation compatibility.

X Event Lab Platform

X Event's lab platform presents the studio's internal R&D efforts and experimental mindset. It functions as a dedicated space for showcasing ongoing research, new technical ideas, and future-facing creative exploration.

For clients and partners, the platform demonstrates how emerging technologies can open up new ways to build more engaging and memorable brand experiences. Its design language balances clarity, curiosity, and innovation.

Services: Concept development, web design, web development, 3D design, WebGL

Highlights: Innovation-focused positioning, experimental showcase structure, and technology-led storytelling.
